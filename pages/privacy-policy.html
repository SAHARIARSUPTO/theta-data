Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our Service. It also explains your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Interpretation and Definitions

Words with capitalized initials have specific meanings as defined below. The same definitions apply whether they appear in singular or plural form.

Definitions

  • Account — A unique account created for you to access our Service.
  • Company — Refers to Theta Data, located at 74 Carpenter Ave.
  • Cookies — Small data files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience.
  • Device — Any device that can access the Service (e.g., computer, smartphone, tablet).
  • Personal Data — Any information that identifies an individual.

Data Collection and Usage

We may collect personal information such as your email, name, phone number, address, and usage data to provide and improve our services.

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

We use Cookies, Web Beacons, and similar technologies to track activity on our website and improve performance. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Use of Your Personal Data

Retention and Security

Your data will be retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. We take reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13 years old. If you believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.
